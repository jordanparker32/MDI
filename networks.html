Professional Networks / Assistance Providers

Mississippi defense industry companies can find support for all of their business and networking needs in the state. Whether assistance is needed in manufacturing aerospace products, research and development for ocean technologies, financing new ventures, networking in industries, and more, Mississippi has the organizations and associations to support these activities. Among these organizations include the Center for Advanced Vehicular Systems (CAVS), The Accelerator, the Mississippi Polymer Institute, and the Aerospace Industries Association. To have your organization included in MDDI’s catalog of resources, please contact us at mddi@usm.edu.
